<p><p>Industrial gases are a group of gases that are produced and used for industrial applications or processes. These gases are used in various industries such as manufacturing, healthcare, food and beverages, chemicals, and electronics, among others. They are used for processes like welding, cutting, cooling, and packaging, as well as for enhancing the efficiency and quality of various industrial processes.</p><p>Specialty gases, on the other hand, are a category of high-purity gases that are used in specific applications that require precision, accuracy, and reliability. These gases are often produced in small quantities and are used in industries such as pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, environmental analysis, and research and development.</p><p>The industrial gases and specialty gases market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years. Various factors are driving this growth, including the increasing demand from end-use industries, technological advancements, and the growing need for efficient and cost-effective industrial processes.</p><p>The healthcare industry, in particular, is expected to drive the demand for industrial and specialty gases.
