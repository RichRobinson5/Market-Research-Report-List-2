Phase I SMOs focus on early-stage trials, which evaluate the safety of the drug. Phase II SMOs manage mid-stage trials, where the drug's efficacy and side effects are examined. Phase III SMOs oversee late-stage trials involving larger groups of patients to collect more data. These different types of SMOs specialize in managing clinical trials at specific stages of drug development.</p></p>
