<p><p>RTV silicone rubber is a type of synthetic polymer material used for various applications. There are two main types in the market: RTV-1 and RTV-2. </p><p>RTV-1, also known as one-component RTV silicone rubber, is a ready-to-use product that does not require any additional mixing. It cures at room temperature when exposed to moisture in the air, forming a flexible and durable rubber-like material. It is commonly used for sealing and bonding applications in electronic, automotive, and construction industries.</p><p>RTV-2, also known as two-component RTV silicone rubber, requires two components (A and B) to be mixed in a defined ratio before curing can occur. It offers more versatility in terms of hardness and attributes like tear resistance, elongation, and dimensional stability. RTV-2 is often used for mold making, prototyping, casting, and other industrial applications.</p></p>
