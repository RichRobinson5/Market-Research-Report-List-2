<p><p>The stud bolts market is divided into four types based on their design and application. First, fully threaded stud bolts have threads along the entire length for versatile use. Second, double ends stud bolts are threaded on both ends only, useful for connecting two components. Third, tap ends stud bolts are designed for use with threaded holes. Finally, step downs stud bolts have different diameter ends, allowing for connections between components with varied diameters. These different types cater to various industrial needs for secure and efficient fastening applications.</p></p>
