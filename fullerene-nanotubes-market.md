<p><p>The Fullerene Nanotubes Market consists of three main types: Single-Wall Nanotubes (SWNT), Double-Wall Nanotubes (DWNT), and Multi-Wall Nanotubes (MWNT). SWNTs are single-layered carbon nanotubes, known for their high strength and electrical conductivity. DWNTs comprise two concentric nanotubes, offering enhanced structural stability and gas storage capacity. MWNTs consist of multiple concentric nanotubes, providing excellent mechanical strength and durability. These different types cater to various industry applications, including electronics, optics, energy storage, and composite materials, due to their unique properties and versatile characteristics.</p></p>
