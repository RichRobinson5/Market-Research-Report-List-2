<p><p>An Electric Double-layer Capacitor (EDLC), also known as a supercapacitor or ultracapacitor, is an energy storage device that uses electrical double layer phenomena to store electrical energy. Unlike traditional capacitors that store energy in an electrostatic field, EDLCs store energy in the form of ions on the surface of an electrode, thereby providing higher energy density and longer cycle life.</p><p>The current outlook for the EDLC market is promising. With the increasing demand for energy storage solutions, especially in renewable energy systems, electric vehicles, and consumer electronics, the EDLC market is expected to witness significant growth.
