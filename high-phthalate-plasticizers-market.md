<p><p>The high phthalate plasticizers market consists of various types, including DINP, DIDP, DPHP, DIUP, and DTDP. DINP (Diisononyl Phthalate) is a widely used plasticizer known for its versatility and low volatility. DIDP (Diisodecyl Phthalate) is preferred for high temperature applications due to its excellent heat stability. DPHP (Di-2-propylheptyl Phthalate) is commonly used in medical devices, cables, and automotive applications. DIUP (Diisoundecyl Phthalate) is mainly utilized in the manufacture of flooring and other building construction materials. Lastly, DTDP (Diisodecyl Terephthalate) is a plasticizer commonly used in the production of PVC products, such as wire and cable insulation.</p></p>
